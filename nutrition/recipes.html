Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Tomato slices
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Mash the avocado and spread it on the toast.
  3. Top with tomato slices, salt, and pepper.

Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa, cooked and cooled
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Cucumber, diced
  • Red bell pepper, chopped
  • Feta cheese, crumbled
  • Black olives, sliced
  • Fresh lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, feta cheese, and black olives.
  2. Drizzle with fresh lemon juice and olive oil.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, then toss to combine.
  4. Refrigerate before serving.

Green Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1/2 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • Ice cubes
  • Honey to taste

Instructions:

  1. Blend spinach, banana, pineapple chunks, Greek yogurt, and almond milk until smooth.
  2. Add ice cubes and blend again until the desired consistency is reached.
  3. Sweeten with honey to taste.

Grilled Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, dried dill, salt, and pepper to create a marinade.
  3. Coat salmon fillets with the marinade and let them sit for 15 minutes.
  4. Grill salmon for 4-5 minutes per side or until cooked through.
